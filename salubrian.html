Salubrian

Salubrian is an automated scheduling platform for medical clinics. Salubrian solves the pain associated with medical appointment wait times and missed appointments using an innovative real-time SMS appointment update service.

Sketches

Problem

Healthcare administration in Canada is lagging behind…. Far behind. It is antiquated and stuck in the age of Windows 95. We know the Canadian healthcare system is inefficient, like many around the world, and we at Salubrian Health on a mission to make our healthcare system more-cost efficient, hack productivity, and more importantly provide better care. Physicians are frustrated with missed and forgotten appointments. In fact, on average, each physician in Canada has two no-shows at day. Not only does this cost revenue, but it also sacrifices patient care due to lost efficiency. Other service industries, disrupted by consumer-oriented technology innovation, like online vacation booking and banking, have reaped huge benefits in reduced overhead and increased satisfaction.... it’s time we brought the benefits of technology to doctor’s offices across Canada for both physicians and patients.

Persona

Physicians, who manage or own small-to-medium size health clinics in Canada with a particular emphasis on urban clinic locations. Research shows our early adopter physicians are tech savvy, definitely entrepreneurial, on the ambitious side (even more so than their peers), and like to indulge in the finer things in life such as good food and golf.
